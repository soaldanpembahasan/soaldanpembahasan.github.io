Jawaban:

1. ( + ) She wanted to go to the cinema yesterday. (Ia ingin pergi ke bioskop kemarin.)

( – ) She did not want to study Math yesterday.

(Ia tidak ingin belajar Matematika kemarin.)

( ? ) Did she want to study Math yesterday? (Apakah dia ingin belajar Matematika kemarin.)

2. ( + ) Last year we went to Malang.

(Tahun lalu kami pergi ke Malang.)

( – ) Last year we didn’t go to Solo.

(Tahun lalu kami tidak pergi ke Solo.)

( ? ) Did you go to Malang last year?

(Apakah kamu pergi ke Malang tahun lalu?)

3. ( + ) I planned my summer holiday last week. (Saya merencanakan liburan musim panas minggu lalu.)

( – ) I didn’t do my Art project last week.

(Saya tidak mengerjakan proyek Kesenian minggu lalu.)

( ? ) Did you do your Art project last week?

(Apa kamu mengerjakan proyek Kesenian minggu lalu?)

4.( + ) When I gave him a candy, he stopped crying.

(Ketika aku memberinya permen, ia berhenti menangis.)

( – ) If I didn’t give him a candy, he would cry. (Jika aku tidak memberinya permen, ia akan menangis..)

( ? ) Why did you give him a candy?

(Mengapa kamu memberinya permen?)

5. ( + ) Teena started school when she was five. (Teena mulai bersekolah ketika ia berusia 5 tahun.)

( – ) Teena didn’t go to school when she was four. (Teena tidak ke sekolah saat ia berusia 4 tahun.)

( ? ) Did Teena start school when she was four? (Apa Teena mulai bersekolah saat ia berusia 4 tahun?)

6.( + ) My English class began at 9.30.

(Kelas bahasa Inggris saya mulai pada pukul 09.30.)

( – ) My English class did not begin at 8.00.

(Kelas bahasa Inggris saya tidak mulai pada pukul 08.00.)

( ? ) Did your English class begin at 9.00?

(Apa kelas bahasa Inggrismu mulai pada pukul 09.00?)

7. ( + ) I ate my lunch an hour ago.

(Saya makan siang sejam yang lalu.)

( – ) I did not eat lunch with friends.

(Saya tidak makan siang bersama teman-teman.)

( ? ) Did you eat lunch with friends?

8. ( + ) Yesterday evening they had dinner with Mr. President. (Kemarin malam mereka makan bersama Bapak Presiden.)

( – ) Yesterday evening they did not have dinner with their teacher. (Kemarin malam mereka tidak makan bersama guru mereka.)

( ? ) Did they have dinner with their teacher yesterday night?

(Apakah kemarin malam mereka makan bersama guru mereka?)

9.( + ) My cat drank chocolate milk two days ago. (Kucing saya minum susu coklat dua hari yang lalu.)

( – ) My cat didn’t drink chocolate milk yesterday. (Kucing saya tidak minum susu coklat kemarin.)

( ? ) Did your cat drink chocolate milk yesterday? (Apakah kucingmu minum susu coklat kemarin?)

10. ( + ) He liked the food in a food stall like this. (Ia menyukai makanan di warung makan seperti ini.)

( – ) He didn’t like the food in an expensive restaurant.

(Ia tidak menyukai makanan di restoran mahal.)

( ? ) Did he like the food in an expensive restaurant?

(Apa ia menyukai makanan di restoran mahal?)

36. paket bhs inggris kls 7 hal 124 125


DESCRIPTIVE TEXT

Descriptive text adalah teks yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan atau memberikan gambaran (deskripsi) mengenai sesuatu atau seseorang. Jadi tujuan dari descriptive text adalah untuk menggambarkan suatu hal.

Ciri-ciri dari descriptive text adalah:

Menggunakan simple presentMenggunakan action verb yaitu kata kerja yang menunjukkan suatu kegiatan yang bisa dilihat, contohnya walk, sleep, read, cook dan lain sebagainyaVerb yang digunakan adalah attribute verb seperti be (is, am and are)

Untuk menanyakan deskripsi/gambaran mengenai seseorang atau sesuatu kalimat tanya yang biasa digunakan adalah :

What is..... like?What does.... look like?

Contohnya:

What is your new teacher like?What does your new house look like?What is it like?

Pembahasan

Situation 1

Lisa: Dayu, do you have a pet?

Dayu: Yes, I do. I have a dog

Lisa: I love dogs! What is it like?

Dayu: It's a he. He has brown curly fur and big round eyes. He is very playful.

Lisa: Wow! I can imagine how adorable he is.

Situation 2

Siti: Dayu, do you often go to the park near your house?

Dayu: Yes,  I do.

Siti: What does the park look like?

Dayu: It's beautiful. There are so  many beautiful flowers and big trees.

Siti: That's great. We should go there together sometime.

Situation 3

Udin: Do you know Farhan Ramadhan?

Dayu: Yes, I do.

Udin: What is he like?

Dayu: He is about your age and has curly hair. He is a nice person to talk to and have a soft voice.

Situation 4

Lisa: Siti, you look so happy today.

Siti: Yes, my mother bought me a new cell phone yesterday.

Lisa: May I see it?

Siti: I want to focus on study at school, so I left it at home.

Lisa: What is your phone like?

Siti: It's white, has a big wide screen and it's very light.
