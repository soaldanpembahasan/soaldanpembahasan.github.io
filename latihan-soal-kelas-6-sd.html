Jawab:
(1a) Volume ≈ 3.885,²³ cm³
(1b) Volume = 985 cm³

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

(1a) Tinggi tabung (t) = 15 cm
Tinggi kerucut (h) = 28-15 = 13 cm
Jari-jari (r) = 8 cm
Volume = kerucut + tabung =
(πr²h÷3) + (πr²t) =
πr²((h÷3)+t) ≈
3,14×8²×((13÷3)+15) ≈
3,14×64×((13÷3)+(45÷3)) ≈
200,96×(58÷3) ≈
11.655,68÷3)
≈ 3.885,²³ cm³

(1b) L = 5 cm, P = 22 cm
T = 8 cm, t = 6 cm
a = 22-5-10
a = 7 cm
Volume = (P×L×T) + (L×a×t/2)
Volume = (22×5×8) + (5×7×6/2)
Volume = (110×8) + (35×3)
Volume = 880 + 105
Volume = 985 cm³
