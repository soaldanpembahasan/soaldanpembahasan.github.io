= (sin 30° + cos 60°) / (tan 45° - (-sin 60°))

= (1/2 + 1/2) / (1 + 1/2 √3)

= 1 / ((2 + √3)/2)

= 2/(2 + √3)

= 2(2 - √3) / (2 + √3)(2 - √3)
