2. petanyaan 08.45 A: what time is it?B: it is ​


Jawaban:

08:45

- It's eight forty-five (American)

- It's (a) quarter to nine (British)

Penjelasan:

TELLING TIMES ⌚

Terdapat dua versi untuk menyatakan waktu dalam bahasa Inggris.

American (Jam + Menit)

∙ 04:10 ➔ It's four ten

∙ 05:25 ➔ It's five twenty-five

∙ 01:09 ➔ It's one o-nine (dibaca o nine)

∙ 02:43 ➔ It's two forty-three

∙ 07:30 ➔ It's seven thirty

British (Menit + Past/To + Jam)

∙ 07:40 ➔ It's twenty to eight

∙ 11:25 ➔ It's twenty-five past eleven

∙ 04:53 ➔ It's seven to five

∙ 08:28 ➔ It's twenty-eight past eight

∙ 05:15 ➔ It's (a) quarter past five

∙ 09:45 ➔ It's (a) quarter to ten

∙ 10:30 ➔ It's half past ten

Note:

￫ Menit 1-29 gunakan past diikuti jam berada

￫ Menit 31-59 gunakan to diikuti jam menuju

￫ Menit ke 15 gunakan (a) quarter past

￫ Menit ke 45 gunakan (a) quarter to

￫ Menit ke 30 gunakan half past

O'clock

Gunakan o'clock jika tidak ada menit.

∙ 02:00 ➔ It's two o'clock

∙ 07:00 ➔ It's seven o'clock
