Simile figure of speech is used to describe a situation by comparing it to something else. metaphor : Metaphor comes from the Greek which means to move. Metaphor is used to help the writer describe things clearly, by comparing one thing with other things that have the same characteristics and characteristics. The difference between metaphor and simile is that metaphorical figure of speech is implicit. While the simile is explicit.personification: Personification of figure of speech is a figure of speech that attaches the characteristics of living things to inanimate objects.Personification or cognition is a style of language that equates objects with humans, having properties, abilities, thoughts, feelings, as possessed and experienced by humans.hyperbole: Hyperbole is a figure of speech that is used to exaggerate something. Pembahasan

Majas adalah suatu gaya bahasa yang berbentuk kiasan/perumpamaan yang digunakan untuk memperindah suatu kalimat baik itu lisan atau pun tertulis dengan memanfaatkan kekayaan bahasa untuk menimbulkan kesan imajinatif bagi penyimak dan pembicaranya. Majas biasanya digunakan pada puisi dan prosa, tetapi lebih banyak digunakan pada puisi.

Jenis/Macam-macam Majas

PERSONIFIKASI. Majas personifikasi ialah gaya bahasa yang melukiskan suatu benda dengan memberikan sifat-sifat manusia kepada benda-benda mati sehingga seolah-olah seperti benda hidup.METAFORA. Majas metafora ialah majas yang melukiskan sesuatu dengan perbandingan langsung dan tepat atas dasar sifat yang sama atau hampir sama.IRONI. Majas ironi adalah majas sindiran yang menyatakan sebaliknya dari apa yang sebenarnya dengan maksud untuk menyindir seseorang.SINISME . Majas sinisme yaitu majas sindiran yang menggunakan kata-kata sebaliknya, seperti ironi tetapi kasar.PARADOKS. Majas ini terlihat seolah-olah ada pertentangan atau majas yang antarbagiannya menyatakan sesuatu yang bertentangan.

15. give the definition by: rhythm,sound,imagery,forms, metaphor ,personification,simile​


Jawaban:

Berikut yaa :

Rhythm : Rhythm is the pattern of sound, silence, and emphasis in a song. In music theory, rhythm refers to the recurrence of notes and rests (silences) in time. When a series of notes and rests repeats, it forms a rhythmic pattern. In addition to indicating when notes are played, musical rhythm also stipulates how long they are played and with what intensity.

Sound : Sound is a type of energy made by vibrations. When an object vibrates, it causes movement in surrounding air molecules. These molecules bump into the molecules close to them, causing them to vibrate as well. This makes them bump into more nearby air molecules.

Imagery : Imagery can be defined as a writer or speaker’s use of words or figures of speech to create a vivid mental picture or physical sensation.

Forms : Form is the shape, visual appearance, or configuration of an object. In a wider sense, the form is the way something happens. Form also refers to: Form (document), a document (printed or electronic) with spaces in which to write or enter data.

Metaphor : A metaphor is a figure of speech that describes an object or action in a way that isn’t literally true, but helps explain an idea or make a comparison.

Personification : Personification is a figure of speech in which an idea or thing is given human attributes and/or feelings or is spoken of as if it were human. Personification is a common form of metaphor in that human characteristics are attributed to nonhuman things. This allows writers to create life and motion within inanimate objects, animals, and even abstract ideas by assigning them recognizable human behaviors and emotions.

Simile : A simile is a figure of speech that directly compares two things. Similes differ from other metaphors by highlighting the similarities between two things using comparison words such as "like", "as", "so", or " than", while other metaphors create an implicit comparison.

22. The song lyrics above use figurative language... *￼IdiomSimileHyperboleMetaphor​


≡ Figurative Language ≡

The song lyrics above use figurative language hyperbole.

Pembahasan

Figurative language–atau majas, jika dalam bahasa Indonesia–adalah salah satu ragam pemanfaatan bahasa yang digunakan untuk memperindah, membuat suatu karya sastra menjadi terkesan lebih hidup.

1. Idiom

Adalah serangkaian kata yang mewakilkan tindakan atau ekspresi tertentu, namun artinya tidak bisa diartikan secara literal ataupun secara harfiah.

contoh:

Spill the tea.

Jika diartikan, menjadi "tumpahkan tehnya" namun arti sebetulnya adalah "beritahu kabar" atau "sampaikan kebenarannya."

2. Simile

Adalah kiasan yang membandingkan atau menyamakan suatu objek dengan objek lainnya, seperti adanya kata "like" atau "as" maupun kiasan dengan degree of comparison.

contoh:

As bright as your future.

Secerah masa depanmu.

3. Hyperbole

Merupakan kiasan berupa melebih-lebihkan dan terkesan tidak masuk akal.

contoh:

I will sail every ocean to find you.

Aku akan melayari setiap samudera untuk menemukanmu.

4. Metaphor

Kiasan yang membandingkan suatu objek dengan objek lain. Mirip dengan simile, namun bedanya metaphor lebih ke menganggap suatu hal seakan menjadi benda lain daripada hanya menyamakan dari sifatnya.

contoh:

Time is money.

"Waktu adalah uang." Menggambarkan bahwasanya waktu itu sangat berharga, sama berharganya dengan uang. Tidak boleh disia-siakan dan harus dimanfaatkan sebaik-baiknya.

24. WHat is the different between simile and metaphor


Penjelasan:

While both similes and metaphors are used to make comparisons, the difference between similes and metaphors comes down to a word. Similes use the words like or as to compare things—“Life is like a box of chocolates.” In contrast, metaphors directly state a comparison—“Love is a battlefield.”

29. identify the hyperbole,metaphor,or simile! then write the meanings!


FIGURE OF SPEECH

1. I'm just a dead man walking tonight

➡ Metaphor ➡ purposeless, in the great trouble.

2. I could sleep for a year

➡ Hyperbole ➡I'm so exhausted.

3. I feel that life is a fashion show

➡ Metaphor➡every person has their own skin color/matter.

4. She is perfect in everyway

➡ Metaphor ➡ she can handle her matter precisely.

5. Waiting on a never ending train at the crossing

➡ Metaphor ➡ waiting to get out.

6. So take every little piece of my heart

➡ Hyperbole ➡ You hurt me a lot.

7. His smile was as bright as the sun

➡ Simile ➡ his smile is stunning.

EXPLANATION

1) Hyperbole

An exaggerative statement for emphasis. In Bahasa, we assume it as the statement "lebay."

2) Simile  

A comparison made between two different things but at least there is a point in common. It’s commonly identified by the words “like”, “as."

3) Metaphor

An implicit simile, the hidden comparison between two distinct things. The equation of two unrelated things is defined based on some characteristics. In metaphor, the connecting words "like", "as" are omitted.

31. puisi dalam bahasa inggria yang mengandung,simile,metaphor,dan personification?


The Daffodils by William Wordsworth

I wandered lonely as a cloud

That floats on high o'er vales and hills,

When all at once I saw a crowd,

A host, of golden daffodils;

Beside the lake, beneath the trees,

Fluttering and dancing in the breeze.

Continuous as the stars that shine

And twinkle on the milky way,

They stretched in never-ending line

Along the margin of a bay:

Ten thousand saw I at a glance,

Tossing their heads in sprightly dance.

The waves beside them danced; but they

Out-did the sparkling waves in glee:

A poet could not but be gay,

In such a jocund company:

I gazed—and gazed—but little thought

What wealth the show to me had brought:

For oft, when on my couch I lie

In vacant or in pensive mood,

They flash upon that inward eye

Which is the bliss of solitude;

And then my heart with pleasure fills,

And dances with the daffodils.
